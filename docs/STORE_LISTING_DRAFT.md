## Full Description

CatDex is a relaxed mobile collection game for cat lovers.

Take or import a cat photo, let CatDex analyze the discovery, and unlock a collectible card with species, rarity, variant, traits, personality, and a cute story.

Build your CatDex over time, earn XP, watch your collection grow, and enjoy a bright, friendly game made for casual play.

## Key Features

- Capture or import cat photos.
- Analyze cat traits through the CatDex discovery flow.
- Unlock collectible cat cards.
- Earn XP and progress your explorer level.
- Browse your CatDex collection.
- Play in guest mode.
- Optional cloud mode when signed in.
